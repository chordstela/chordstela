6. Did you … (see) Danielo last summer? a. sawb. seedc. seesd. see​


Jawaban: A. Saw (Salah) D. See (Benar)

Penjelasan: Yang dikatakan komen benar, maaf sudah menyesatkan

Jawaban :

d. see

Penjelasan :

(+) Subject – Verb 2 – Time Signals.

(-) Subject did not – Verb 1 – Time Signals.

(?) Did – Subject – Verb 1 – Time Signals?

Example :

(+) He visited a movie studio yesterday.

(-) He did not visit a movie studio yesterday.

(?) Did he visit a movie studio yesterday?

Yes, he did./ No, he didn’t.

8. Example : I / not / play / tennis / last summer ------ I haven’t played tennis since last summer. 1. I / know / her / more than ten years. 2. I / not / eat / anything / lunchtime. 3. You / live / in this town / a long time? 4. Jill / be / a good friend / we were at school together. 5. You / see / Jack / the party last week?


Jawaban

→ dengan perintah penggunaan present perfect

1. I haven't known her 'for' more than ten years

2. I haven't eaten anything 'since' lunchtime

3. Have you lived in this town 'for' a long time?

4. Jill has been a good friend 'when' we were at school together

5. Have you seen Jack 'on' the party last week

Penjelasan:

Penggunaan present perfect menerangkan kalimat dengan keadaan pada saat kini dalam keberadaan benar-benar terjadi

Penggunaan:

S + has/have + V3 + O/C (+)

S + has/have + not + V3 + O/C (-)

has/have + S + V3 + O/C (?)

Dengan verba dalam bentuk V3 dan has untuk he, she, it, name dan have untuk I, you, they, we, names
