15. 1.how many seasons are there in Indonesia? 2.when is the rainy season?3.it's usually very cool it refers to... 4.does the sun shine brightly in rainy season? 5.how are the rivers during the dry season?


Subject : English

Category : Reading Comprehension

Level : JHS VII

Code : 7.5

Keywords : season

---------

1. How many seasons are there in Indonesia?  

There are two seasons in Indonesia.

bisa dilihat di kalimat pertama paragraf pertama.

2. When is the rainy season?

The rainy season is in November to April.

Karena dry season dari bulan Mei sampai Oktober (par. 1 kalimat ke-4) maka sisanya adalah rainy season.

3. It's usually very cool ...  

It refers to the temperature during the rainy season.

Karena yang sejuk adalah suhu (temperature) pada musim hujan.

4. Does the sun shine brightly in rainy season?  

No, it doesn't.

Par 2 : ... and the sky is cloudy. Cloudy = berawan.

5. How are the rivers during the dry season?

The rivers do not have enough water and some rivers are even dry.

Par 2 kalimat pertama : the rivers do not have ...

26. in the dry season,the weat feels hot because......a. the sun shines every dayb. the sun shines every clear c. the rain falls down every dayd. the weather feels windy​


Jawaban:

A. The sun shines every day

QUESTION

In the dry season, the wheat feels hot because...

ANSWER

A. the sun shines everyday

EXPLANATION

Q : In the dry season, the wheat feels hot because...

Indo : Di musim kemarau, gandum terasa panas karena...

A. the sun shines everydayA. matahari bersinar setiap hari

B. the sun shines every clear saya bingung arti dari kalimat ini

C. the rain falls down everydayC. hujan turun setiap hari

D. the weather feels windyD. cuacanya terasa berangin

Mengapa A?

Musim kemarau tidak mungkin turun hujan. (Pilihan C sangat tidak mungkin terjadi saat kemarau)Gandum terasa hangat/panas (the wheat feels hot) karena terpapar sinar matahari yang begitu lama. (Pilihan A adalah jawabannya)Jika cuaca berangin (the weather feels windy), gandum tidak akan terasa panas karena di tiup angin. (Pilihan D bukanlah jawaban yang tepat)Untuk pilihan B, saya tidak mengerti kalimatnya. Saya tidak tahu apa arti 'every clear'. Mungkin yang dimaksud 'very clear'?
