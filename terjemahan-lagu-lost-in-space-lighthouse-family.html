35. 44. For the first time in 11 years, Fifth Avenue in New York, USA, lost expensive area in the world in which to rent shop space. A. it B. its C. the D. their E. theirs​


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

The correct answer is A. it.

The sentence "For the first time in 11 years, Fifth Avenue in New York, USA, lost expensive area in the world in which to rent shop space" indicates that Fifth Avenue lost its status as the most expensive area in the world. The possessive pronoun "its" is used to show that the expensive area belonged to Fifth Avenue. Therefore, the correct choice is "it."
