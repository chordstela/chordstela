25. Rubah ke passive voice Mila has been waiting for you Ke dua My father repaired the bridge


the bridge was repaired by my father

Untuk Present Perfect  Continuous Tense
Active : S+ have/has + been + V1-ing + O
Pasif : S + have/has + been + being + V3 + by + O

Untuk simple past tense
Active: S + V2 + O
Passive: S + was/were + V3 + by + O

1.
Active: Mila has been waiting for you.
Passive: You have been being waited by Mila.

2.
Active: My father repaired the bridge.
Passive: The bridge was repaired by my father.

A. have been waiting

soal diatas merupakan kalimat Present Perfect Continuous Tense, dengan formula:

S + has/have + been + Ving

(The passengers have been waiting) the passengers itu plural oleh karena itu memakai have. Keterangan waktu since this morning menandakan Present Perfect Continuous Tense.
