23. Listeni your father .... to a friend right no


Jawab: is talking (sedang berbicara)

Listen! your father [is talking] to a friend right now

(Dengar! Ayahmu sedang berbicara Prada seorang tenant sekarang)

Semoga membantu

Catatan:

Berdasarkan soal, Ada keterangan waktu Right now. Mala rumus Present Continues yg digunakan:

(+) S + to be (is/am/are) + V+ing + O

(-) S + to be (is/am/are) + not + V+ing + O

(?) to be (is/am/are) + S + V+ing + O ?

Semoga membantu
